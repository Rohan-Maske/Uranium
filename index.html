What is Potion and how does it work?

Potion is an advanced video generation AI. It allows you to create AI personalized videos in your own face and voice at scale. The AI technology learns how you speak, emote, and gesture when you talk and then creates videos on your behalf. Potion videos are proven to reduce effort in video creation and enhance engagement on sales and marketing campaigns.

What is the difference between self-recorded and AI-generated videos in Potion?

Self-recorded videos allow you to personally create video content, adding a human touch to your outreach. AI-generated videos, on the other hand, use text input to create videos automatically with your face & voice, enabling you to scale your video production without needing to record each one individually. Both options can be personalized, depending on your outreach strategy.

Yes, we synchronize gestures with the audio in your video, ensuring that the avatar’s movements are in harmony with the speech. This synchronization helps create a more cohesive and engaging viewer experience.
